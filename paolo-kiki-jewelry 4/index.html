What We Do

Services of distinction

Every piece of jewelry carries a story. We offer the full range of services to create, restore, and perfect yours.

01

Diamond Setting

Prong, bezel, pavé, channel, and invisible setting — stones placed with precision to maximize brilliance and security.

02

Custom Design

From concept sketch to finished piece, we craft original jewelry tailored to your exact vision and specifications.

03

Repair & Restoration

Broken clasps, bent prongs, worn shanks — cherished pieces restored to their original beauty with exceptional workmanship.

04

Ring Sizing

Precise sizing for all metals, including platinum and eternity bands. Your ring should fit as perfectly as it looks.

05

Stone Sourcing

We help source ethically obtained diamonds and gemstones that match your specific quality, cut, and budget requirements.

06

Polishing & Cleaning

Professional ultrasonic cleaning and hand polishing to restore the original luster of gold, silver, and platinum pieces.
